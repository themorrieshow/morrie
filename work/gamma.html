BCGX Retail AI

Lead UX Designer on 6 retail AI engagements over 2.5 years. Designed end-to-end tooling that helps enterprise pricing teams extract maximum value from every sale using AI-driven price optimization.

Client Nordstrom, Walgreens & more
Industry Retail / AI
Outcome Increased sales, reduced waste, reduced aging inventory
The Business Problem

Businesses are not correctly leveraging all the data at their disposal in pricing decisions. With the ability to fine-tune a product's list price and discount schedule, they will extract the most value possible out of each sale. Pricing accuracy matters most for high-volume businesses and/or businesses with small profit margins (economy pricing).

My Role

In my role as Lead UX Designer at BCG GAMMA, I partnered with client teams to lead them through an end-to-end design process — beginning with user research to shed light on their pricing processes — and ending by collaborating with engineering and data science to build tools for their enterprise pricing teams.
