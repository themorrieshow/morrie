Allstate – Avail Carsharing

Integrated P2P car-sharing platform supporting airport rentals and community car-sharing. Built for Allstate to pilot new insurance products and adapt as car ownership culture evolves toward autonomous vehicles.

Client Allstate
Industry Transportation / Insurance
Outcome Launch in market
Business Objective

Avail is Allstate's integrated P2P car-sharing platform, built to support multiple use cases including airport rentals and community car-sharing. Beyond the product itself, Avail was designed to serve as a strategic testing ground — a live environment where Allstate could experiment with alternative car ownership models, explore emerging insurance products, and build institutional knowledge ahead of larger shifts in how people own and access vehicles.
